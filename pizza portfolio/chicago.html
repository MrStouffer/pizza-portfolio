The dish on Chicago's pie

The makings of a deep dish pizza
Chicago-style pizza is pizza prepared according to several different styles developed in Chicago, widely referred to simply as deep dish pizza due to its cooking style. The pan in which it is baked gives the pizza its characteristically high edge which provides ample space for large amounts of cheese and a chunky tomato sauce. Chicago-style pizza may be prepared in deep-dish style and as a stuffed pizza
History of the deep dish pie
Many claim to be the creator of the deep dish pizza but the truth is the orgin story of this dish is just as illusive as it is delicious. The orgins of the deep dish pie can be traced back to the early 1900s but the true identiy of the inventor has been lost to time.
Popularity
Chicago-style has established itself as one of the most popular types of pizza in America. Hundreds of pizzarias across America strive to create their own version of deep dish pizza. This style of pizza is deeply rooted in the culture of our country, it is as American as apple pie.
